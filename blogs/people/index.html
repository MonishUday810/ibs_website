Important People to Follow
Posted on August 6, 2025 · Written by Team IBS

In today’s fast-paced world, children and parents need positive role models who can inspire values, learning, and resilience. This list brings together influential personalities whose journeys and insights can help families stay motivated and focused on growth. We recommend these people because their stories embody qualities like perseverance, humility, and creativity—values that can shape both personal and academic success for children and encourage mindful parenting.

1. Sudha Murthy
A renowned author and philanthropist, Sudha Murthy’s stories are filled with warmth and life lessons. She inspires children through her simple yet powerful tales and encourages parents to nurture values of kindness and humility. Her work emphasizes empathy and simplicity, making her relatable to young minds. Follow her to explore stories that combine tradition with modern life lessons.
2. APJ Abdul Kalam
Known as the “Missile Man of India” and former President, Dr. Kalam’s life and words continue to inspire generations. Children can learn the value of dreams and perseverance, while parents can guide them to aim high and stay grounded. His books and speeches motivate families to embrace science and technology for a better future. Following his legacy is a constant reminder to stay humble while achieving greatness.
3. Malala Yousafzai
A global advocate for education, Malala’s courage and determination remind children of the power of voice and education. Parents can follow her work to understand how to foster equality and resilience in their children. Her fight for education rights shows that age is never a barrier to creating change. She inspires families to value learning as the most powerful tool for progress.
4. Ratan Tata
An iconic leader and philanthropist, Ratan Tata exemplifies humility and generosity. His story motivates children to value ethics and parents to teach purpose beyond profit. He has shown how business can be driven by compassion and integrity, not just profits. Families can learn from his leadership about kindness, social responsibility, and making a positive impact.
5. Mary Kom
A world champion boxer and a mother, Mary Kom demonstrates perseverance and strength. She shows children that determination can overcome any obstacle and inspires parents to support their child’s passion. Her journey from humble beginnings to global recognition teaches resilience. Families can draw inspiration from her story to never give up on dreams.
